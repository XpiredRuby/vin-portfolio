GHOST

GPS-Denied Hardware Occlusion-Survivable Tracker Active development

Active development

Vision-based state estimation for GPS-denied environments. Dual-filter architecture combining a 9-state camera platform attitude ESKF with AprilTag vision measurements and a CV/CTRV/IMM target tracking filter — running on Raspberry Pi 4B with PX4 SITL over UDP MAVLink.
